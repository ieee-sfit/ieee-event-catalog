GAME

A physical 10 x 10 grid. Two teams of 4 play against each other. Players place 5 ships anywhere on the grid.

  • Ship Lengths:
    • 1 of length 5
    • 1 of length 4
    • 1 of length 2
    • 2 of length 3
  • Time limit: 30 seconds for each team to hit.
  • Use of Powerup uses one action point.
  • Powerup can be used only once per turn.
  • Red(Outline & Text): Difficult
    Orange(Outline & Text): Medium
    [Powerups Section Below]
  • Character ability can be used every third round.

HOW TO PLAY

  • Each player has a 10x10 grid to place their ships.
  • Players place their fleet of 5 ships on the grid. Ships can be placed horizontally or vertically but not diagonally.
  • Ships cannot overlap and must be placed within the grid boundaries.
  • Players take turns calling out coordinates (e.g., "B7") to attack the opponent's ships.
  • The opponent announces whether the shot is a "hit" or a "miss".
  • Players mark their hits and misses on a separate grid to keep track of their guesses.
  • A ship is sunk when all its coordinates have been hit.
  • The game continues until one player has sunk all of the opponent's ships.
